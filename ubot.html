Project overwiew
The YOUX Systems team partnered with a client to create a smart bot in the Telegmram messenger that enabled the creation and management of online shops. Using a mobile device, customers placed orders and consultants used a tablet to take them into action. In the sales console, cashiers made the sale and sent the completed order to the cash register. For some online shops, a hybrid scenario was used in which the roles of consultant and cashier were combined.
